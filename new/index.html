Fish

A fish is an amazing animal which lives and breathes in water. Fish have been on the Earth for over 500 million years. All fish have a backbone and most breathe through gills and have fins and scales. Fish have excellent senses of sight, touch, taste and many possess a good sense of smell and ‘hearing’.

Cat
